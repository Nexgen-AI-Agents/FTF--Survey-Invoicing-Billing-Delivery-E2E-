Step 1 — Trigger
Scan for $ Flagged Orders
Every 15 minutes, query the FTF system for all orders carrying the $ (invoice_needed) flag. Orders already processed are skipped — only new ones are queued.

Step 2 — Data Gathering
Gather Client Requirements
For each flagged order, AI searches all 3 sources simultaneously to find what the client needs on the invoice — services, quantities, billing details.

Step 3 — Invoice Preparation
Prepare & Price Invoice
AI extracts all service details from the gathered requirements. Pricing is validated against the last 2 years of invoice history for similar jobs in the same area. If price is not justified for the current year or market, AI suggests a correction before sending for approval.

Step 4 — Human Gate
Request Approval via Teams
Invoice details + order # are posted to the Teams group chat. Ryan / Robert / Prateek review and reply. If questions arise, AI answers in Teams, modifies the invoice, and re-requests approval. No invoice is created until approved.
