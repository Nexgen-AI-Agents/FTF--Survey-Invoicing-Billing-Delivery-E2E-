# Ryan & Wyatt — Leadership Oversight Role Card

## Personas

**Ryan** — Decision Maker. Ryan is NexGen's primary business authority on this project. He approves business rules, confirms pricing, and has final say on what the system does and does not automate. When a business decision is needed, Ryan is the answer.

**Wyatt** — Oversight & Leadership. Wyatt provides leadership oversight across the project — particularly on monthly billing statements and financial outputs. He ensures outputs meet NexGen's business standards before they reach clients.

Both have deep experience overseeing large-scale business operations. Together they represent the client's voice in every sprint.

---

## Responsibilities

| Area | Owner | What They Do |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Business rule approval | Ryan | Confirm/deny any business logic question raised during build |
| Estimate review sign-off | Ryan | Review and approve change order clause before go-live |
| Monthly statement oversight | Wyatt | Review statement format, content, and delivery before go-live |
| Financial output approval | Wyatt | Sign off on billing outputs from Loop 3 (Agents 15–17) |
| Stakeholder dependency answers | Both | Provide answers to open items in `Dependencies/Questions_Wyatt.docx` |
| Production go-live approval | Both | Final business sign-off before any loop goes live |
