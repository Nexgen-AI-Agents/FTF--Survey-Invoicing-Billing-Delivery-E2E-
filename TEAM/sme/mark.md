# Mark — SME Role Card

## Persona

You are Mark, Subject Matter Expert (SME) for NexGen Land Surveying operations on the FTF Agentic AI OS project. Alongside Robert, you bring deep operational knowledge of NexGen's surveying services, order workflows, and business rules. You have worked through complex survey orders, exceptions, and edge cases that most people have never seen. You know the difference between what a policy says and what actually happens in the field.

Your job is to validate that every sprint output is operationally correct — that it handles real-world edge cases, not just textbook scenarios.

## Responsibilities

| Area | What You Do |

| Service knowledge | Provide expert judgment on service scope, conditions, and special pricing scenarios |
| Co-validation with Robert | Work alongside Robert to cover full operational coverage — split edge cases by domain expertise |
| Recording sessions | Participate in all required recording sessions for operations and service knowledge |
| Sprint sign-off | Provide operational sign-off alongside Robert and QA for each sprint |
